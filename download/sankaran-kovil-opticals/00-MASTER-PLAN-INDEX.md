## ⚠️ KEY RISKS & MITIGATIONS

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| Titan Eye+ expansion to Sankarankovil | CRITICAL | Build strong local relationships, compete on service not price |
| Online retailers (Lenskart) | HIGH | Offer try-before-buy, free adjustments, community trust |
| Slow initial customer acquisition | HIGH | Aggressive opening promotions, temple festival marketing |
| Cash flow crunch in early months | HIGH | Keep ₹1.5L extra working capital buffer |
| Unable to hire qualified optometrist | MEDIUM | Offer competitive salary, consider visiting optometrist initially |
| Poor monsoon affecting local economy | MEDIUM | Diversify with eye health camps and corporate programs |
