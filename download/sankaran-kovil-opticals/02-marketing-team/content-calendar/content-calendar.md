## 1. Executive Summary

This document provides a **comprehensive, actionable 12-month content calendar** for Sankaran Kovil Opticals — a trusted optical retail shop serving Sankarankovil and surrounding areas in southern Tamil Nadu. The calendar is built on three strategic pillars:

- **Festival-Driven Engagement** — Leveraging Tamil Nadu's rich festival calendar (Pongal, Thai Poosam, Navaratri, Diwali, Ayudha Puja) to create culturally resonant marketing campaigns.
- **Eye Health Authority** — Positioning the shop as the go-to local expert through awareness content aligned with global and national eye health observances (World Sight Day, Glaucoma Awareness Month, World Optometry Day).
- **Seasonal Relevance** — Capitalizing on Tamil Nadu's seasonal patterns (March–June sunglasses peak, June back-to-school, October–December festive gifting, January Pongal new beginnings).

**Total planned content:** ~260+ posts per year (minimum 20 per month) across Instagram, Facebook, Google Business Profile, WhatsApp, and blog.

### 2.3 Seasonal Optical Shopping Trends in India (Tamil Nadu Context)

**Peak Sunglasses Season: March–July**
- Tamil Nadu's summer (March–June) sees intense UV exposure (UV index often exceeds 10)
- Chennai/southern TN temperatures reach 40°C+ in April–May
- Outdoor workers, farmers, two-wheeler riders are key demographics
- Sunglasses account for 35–45% of optical retail revenue in summer months

**Back-to-School Rush: May–July**
- Tamil Nadu schools reopen in early June
- May is the pre-purchase window for school supplies including eyewear
- Children's eye exams spike 200%+ in May–June
- Blue-cut lenses and lightweight frames are top sellers

**Festive Gifting Season: September–January**
- Navaratri (Sep/Oct) → Diwali (Oct) → Christmas (Dec) → Pongal (Jan)
- Eyewear is increasingly popular as a gift item
- Premium frame sales increase 50–70% during Diwali
- Contact lens trials peak during festive periods

**Wedding Season: November–February**
- Tamil wedding season brings demand for designer frames
- Photo-ready eyewear and contact lenses are popular
- Bridal party styling consultations

**Monsoon/Low Season: July–September**
- Indoor screen time increases → blue-cut lens opportunity
- Eye infection awareness content is relevant
- Good time for appointment-based eye exams

### 2.4 Eyewear Market Trends 2026

- **Indian eyewear market** valued at ~$11 billion (2025), projected to reach $20.86 billion by 2034
- **Key trends:** Oversized frames, geometric shapes (hexagon), slim profiles, sustainable materials
- **Growing demand:** Blue-cut/digital protection lenses, photochromic lenses, lightweight titanium frames
- **Consumer shift:** Eyewear moving from "medical necessity" to "fashion accessory" — especially among 18–35 age group
