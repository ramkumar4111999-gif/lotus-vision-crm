## 2. Footfall Estimation by Category

### A. Bus Commuters Passing Through (THE LARGEST CONTRIBUTOR)

**Reasoning:**
- The New Bus Stand is directly adjacent to this location on Thiruvenkadam Salai.
- 103+ bus services are listed for Sankarankovil on Justdial.
- Key routes: Tirunelveli (6 AC + ~15-20 mofussil buses), Coimbatore (20+ services), Rajapalayam, Sivakasi, Madurai, Chennai (SETC), Tenkasi, Virudhunagar, Thoothukudi, etc.
- Estimated total bus arrivals + departures: **250-300 bus movements per day**.
- Average occupancy per bus: 35-45 passengers (mofussil), 45-55 (express/AC).
- Total daily bus passenger throughput: **~10,000-14,000 passengers/day**.
- This location sits on the main road directly connecting the bus stand to the rest of town. **45-55% of passengers** walk past this spot (to reach auto stands, commercial area, temple side).
- Duplicate factor: Many passengers pass twice (arrival + departure) = 0.7x unique multiplier.

| Estimate | Calculation |
|----------|-------------|
| Low | 10,000 × 45% × 0.7 = **3,150** |
| Medium | 12,000 × 50% × 0.7 = **4,200** |
| High | 14,000 × 55% × 0.7 = **5,390** |

> **Daily bus commuter footfall: 3,150 – 5,400 persons**

- Estimated **35% of temple visitors** pass through this commercial stretch (visiting shops/silk showrooms before or after darshan).

## 7. Key Assumptions & Limitations

1. **Floating population of 15,000/day** is from an official TNUIFSL document and is considered reliable.
2. **Bus frequency** is estimated from multiple sources (Justdial listings, TNSTC timetables, MakeMyTrip). Exact counts unavailable; estimated 250-300 movements/day.
3. **Temple daily visitors** of 2,000-4,000 is extrapolated from the significance of the temple (900 years old, major festivals with thousands) and benchmarked against similar Tamil Nadu temple towns.
4. **Deduplication factor of 0.65-0.75** accounts for overlap — a bus commuter visiting the temple and shopping at Shanthi Complex is counted in categories A, B, and D but is one person passing the point.
5. **School enrollment** is estimated as complete data was unavailable; StackSchools showed only partial class data.
6. **No on-ground count was conducted** — these are desk research-based estimates. Actual counts may vary by ±20-30%.
7. **Festival day spikes** can be 3-5x normal daily footfall, especially during Aadi Thabasu final day and Chittirai Therottam (car festival).
