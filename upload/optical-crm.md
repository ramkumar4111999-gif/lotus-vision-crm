# Optical Shop CRM — Sankarankovil, Tamilnadu

> **Location:** Sankarankovil, Tamilnadu  
> **Goal:** Keep customer data & business operations updated in real-time

---

## 1. Customer Management

- Add / Edit / Delete customer profiles
- Fields: Name, Phone, Email, DOB, Address, Aadhar (optional)
- Spectacle power history (left eye / right eye — SPH, CYL, AXIS, PD)
- Customer visit history with date & notes
- Loyalty points tracking
- Customer group / tags (e.g. "Regular", "Wholesale", "New")

## 2. Product / Inventory Management

- Frames (brand, model, color, size, price, stock)
- Lenses (type: single vision, bifocal, progressive, blue-cut, photochromic)
- Contact lenses (brand, power, duration, stock)
- Sunglasses, solutions, accessories
- Low-stock alerts & auto reorder suggestions
- Barcode / SKU generation & scanning
- Batch expiry tracking (for solutions / lenses)

## 3. Sales & Billing

- Create invoice with customer & items
- GST billing (CGST / SGST / IGST)
- Discounts, offers, & scheme management
- Multiple payment modes: Cash, Card, UPI, Credit
- Print bill / digital copy / WhatsApp share
- Sales return / exchange handling
- Daily, monthly, yearly sales report

## 4. Prescription & Lab Order Management

- Store scanned prescription images
- Send order to lab with lens details & frame
- Track lab order status: Sent → In Progress → Ready → Delivered
- Lab due date reminder
- Cost & margin calculation per order

## 5. Appointment & Reminder System

- Eye checkup appointment booking
- SMS / WhatsApp reminders (e.g. "Your specs are ready", "Yearly checkup due")
- Follow-up reminder for collection
- Calendar view for daily appointments

## 6. Accounting & Finance

- Daily cash flow (inflow / outflow)
- Expense tracking (rent, salary, electricity, lab bills)
- Profit & loss report
- Credit / Due tracking per customer
- Vendor payment tracking
- Bank / UPI transaction log

## 7. Reports & Analytics

- Best selling frames & lenses
- Monthly revenue trend
- Customer acquisition rate
- Top customers by spend
- Inventory turnover report
- Staff performance (if multi-staff)

## 8. Staff / Multi-User Support

- Role-based access: Owner, Manager, Staff
- Staff login with activity log
- Commission tracking per staff (per sale)
- Staff attendance & shift management

## 9. Communication & Marketing

- Bulk SMS / WhatsApp broadcast (offers, reminders)
- Birthday / anniversary auto greeting
- Promotional campaign management
- Customer feedback & rating collection

## 10. Backup & Sync

- Auto cloud backup (daily)
- Export data to Excel / PDF
- Multi-device sync (mobile + desktop)
- Offline support with sync when online

## 11. Extra Features (Nice to Have)

- Multi-language support (Tamil / English)
- Dark mode UI
- Voice search in Tamil (for customer lookup)
- QR-based customer profile sharing
- AI-based frame suggestion based on face shape
- Digital eye test integration
- Online store / catalog for frames
- UPI payment link generation in bill
