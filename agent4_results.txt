6. ACCESSIBILITY CHALLENGES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  ⚠️ KEY CHALLENGES FOR MELA BAZAR LOCATION:

  1. NARROW STREETS: Mela Bazar is the old market area with narrow lanes
     - Difficult for customers arriving by car
     - Two-wheeler parking may be the only option
     - Loading/unloading for stock delivery could be problematic

  2. NOT ON MAIN HIGHWAY: Unlike Tirunelveli Road shops that get pass-through
     traffic from buses and vehicles, Mela Bazar is in the interior
     - No highway visibility or impulse walk-ins from highway traffic
     - Customer must specifically intend to visit Mela Bazar

  3. VISIBILITY: Being on 15B, Shankar Nagar - likely a lane/building number
     - Shop signage visibility may be limited
     - Will need prominent signboard and local awareness marketing

  4. PARKING: Old municipality area likely has no dedicated parking
     - Customers may need to park 100-200 meters away and walk
     - Could deter elderly customers or those with mobility issues

  5. COMPETITION FROM EXISTING OPTICAL SHOPS: 15+ shops already established
     - Many have years of customer relationships and loyalty
     - Price wars likely if too many shops compete for same customer base
